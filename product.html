The Morocan Chicken Pastila

Experience the exquisite flavors of Morocco with our Chicken Pastilla. This savory-sweet pie combines tender chicken, almonds, and aromatic spices wrapped in a delicate, crispy pastry.

Ingredients

Chicken, almonds, cinnamon, saffron, onions, parsley, coriander, eggs, filo pastry, powdered sugar, and spices.

Portion Size

Serves 2-3 people

Nutritional Information

Approx. 450 calories per serving
